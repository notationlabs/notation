Problem: packages/reconciler/src/reconciler.ts:createStepRunner.run retried forever when a step threw RetryableError, so a transient provider outage could hang deploy with no bound. I chose to honor RetryableError.maxAttempts and RetryableError.retryInterval because those fields are the explicit retry contract in yieldstar, and bounded retries make failure modes deterministic. Grounding: commit a06d997 adds attempt counting + sleep in createStepRunner.run and adds a step-runner regression test in packages/reconciler/test/step-runner.test.ts.
